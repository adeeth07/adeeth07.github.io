## About

I am an Associate Scientist - Environmental and Resource Economist in the Sustainable Agrifood Systems Program at the [International Maize and Wheat Improvement Center (CIMMYT)](https://www.cimmyt.org/), based at the CIMMYT office on the ICRISAT campus in Hyderabad, India.

My research focuses on the economics of carbon farming, agricultural carbon credit markets, climate-smart agriculture, and sustainable food systems. I examine whether carbon crediting projects are financially viable, environmentally credible, and socio-economically inclusive, with particular attention to the trade-offs between agricultural productivity, emissions reduction, and rural livelihoods.

Previously, I worked as a Post-Doctoral Fellow at CIMMYT, a Young Professional in the Agriculture and Allied Sectors Vertical of [NITI Aayog](https://www.niti.gov.in/), and a Consultant - Dairy Market for the [Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations](https://www.fao.org/about/en/). I hold a Ph.D. from ICAR-National Dairy Research Institute, Karnal.
